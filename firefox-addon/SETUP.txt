TradeTool POE2 Firefox Bridge

This add-on lets TradeTool send a teleport-to-hideout whisper through a logged-in
Firefox tab on pathofexile.com. TradeTool never receives Firefox cookies.

Side-load for local testing:

1. Open Firefox and go to about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox.
2. Click Load Temporary Add-on.
3. Select firefox-addon/manifest.json from this project.
4. Open the add-on preferences.
5. Copy the Port and Key shown in TradeTool's Firefox TP panel.
6. Keep a logged-in https://www.pathofexile.com/ tab open.
7. Search in TradeTool and press TP on a listing that has teleport support.

The add-on polls http://127.0.0.1:{port}/next with the pairing key, receives only
a one-time hideout token from TradeTool memory, and posts the official
/api/trade2/whisper request from the logged-in Firefox page context.
